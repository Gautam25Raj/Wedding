The Wedding Day

Our wedding day event order as follows with different cultures and religions will incorporate other elements or swap things around.

23.11.2020

25.11.2020

Mehndi

2.00 PM onwards

@Home

Mehendi is the most awaited wedding ritual that every bride waits for it. With tons of fun and enjoyment, mehendi symbolizes love and purity.

Sangeet

6.00 PM onwards

@Home

Among the many pre-wedding events that occur prior to the Hindu, Punjabi, and Gujurati wedding ceremonies, the Sangeet is where the party begins.Sangeet translates to 'sung together' from Sanskrit. 'Gaun', another word used to describe the event, means 'songs' or 'to sing' in Hindi.

Haldi

6.00 PM onwards

@Home

The Haldi ceremony is a ritual holy bath also known as pithi ceremony, which is one of the pre-wedding ceremonies in India. Turmeric (haldi), oil and water are applied to both the bride and groom by married women on the morning of the wedding.

Marriage

7.00 PM onwards

The processional begins with the grandparents, flows through the parents, groom, officant, wedding party, flower girl, and ring bearer, and ends with the bride making her entrance

Wedding Cake

A wedding cake is the traditional cake served at wedding receptions following dinner.The wedding cake is served at a wedding breakfast, the 'wedding breakfast' does not mean the meal will be held in the morning before the wedding.

Wedding Party

7.00 PM onwards

The wedding party the officiant introduces the married couple for the first time. Then, the bride and groom lead the recessional back up the aisle as guests shower you with the rice!
